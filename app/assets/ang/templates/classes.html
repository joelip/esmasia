SAT Seminars

About the course

Unlike most test prep classes, ESM's SAT seminar uses two instructors. The instructors comprehensively identify each student's strengths and weaknesses and then provide one-on-one instruction for each student in every session in the areas each student needs most to boost his or her scores. To learn more about the mentors who will be teaching the courses, please visit our Mentor page

Referral Program

ESM's Referral Program rewards both parents and students if they refer a student who registers for any of our programs.

Parents

$50 off the price of enrollment (or a $50 refund if your student has registered). The referred student is provided with the same discount.

Students

$15 gift card to venues around the area such as Chipotle, Jamba, Starbucks, Pinkberry or a $15 Visa gift card for any student referred.
